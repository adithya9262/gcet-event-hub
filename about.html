Welcome to GCET Event Hub

GCET Event Hub is the official event management platform of Geetanjali College of Engineering and Technology. Our platform empowers students to discover, register, and participate in a wide range of college events — from tech conferences and hackathons to cultural fests, workshops, and sports meets.

We believe that college life is not just about academics. Extracurricular events play a vital role in developing leadership, teamwork, communication, and technical skills. That's why we've built a platform that makes event discovery and registration seamless and accessible to every student.

Whether you want to compete in a hackathon, perform on stage, attend a workshop, or cheer your department in a sports meet — GCET Event Hub has got you covered!

Our Journey

Key milestones in our event management journey

2024 — Platform Launched

GCET Event Hub was conceptualized and launched as a PBL project to streamline event management at our college.

2024 — First 10 Events

Successfully organized and managed 10+ events within the first semester, including workshops and hackathons.

2025 — 1000+ Registrations

Crossed 1000 student registrations across all events, becoming the go-to platform for event management at GCET.

2026 — Going Strong

2500+ registrations, 50+ events, and counting! Now includes admin panel, gallery, and certificate system.
